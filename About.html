About

Esgela offers online applications at the moment only for king Sebata Daliwengo(KSD) Tertiary Institutions.

ESGELA was started early September 2021. ESGELA noted that students at KSD colleges (Eastern Cape) Still use the manual way of appliying which is to go to colleges for the form, print, and fill it manually and send it back. Due to the challenges both colleges and students face. we produced the website that will be advantageous for students and these colleges.

We Esgela appreciate your visit this means you are concerned about your future. Kindly note that at the moment we provide free applications. In case you come across a problem don't hesitate to Contact Us. Thank you!
